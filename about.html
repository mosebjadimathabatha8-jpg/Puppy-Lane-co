Organisation Overview

Puppy Lane & Co is a local spar in polokwane that was founded in 2025 in order to promote our services online and be able to process orders more efficiently and give a welcoming way to interact with current
and previous customers so that they can entrust us with a piece of their family. It was done in order to groom and pemper dogs and planning to introduce a spar that accepts all animals in the new future
This business was started by a young enterprenuer who's goal is to build a bigger business that provides comfort and care for pets for the people of South Africa

Mission: To show your pets the best love and care with the world's finest pampering

Vision: A world where every dog and eventually every pet enjoys wellness and relaxation

Target Audience: Every pet owner who values their pets can feel confident in the care they provide for them
